About the Class

Every Friday morning, Moran leads the full Primary Series with traditional Sanskrit count. The class moves as one — breath synchronized, gaze steady. Moran walks the room offering quiet adjustments while maintaining the count.


Beginners are welcome; she'll guide you through modifications. The session closes with a short led closing sequence and 10 minutes of rest. Bring your own mat. The shala opens at 6:45.

Your Teacher

Moran Ezra
KPJAYI student Yoga Shala Tel Aviv 10+ years

Moran has dedicated over a decade to Ashtanga Yoga. She has made multiple extended trips to Mysore to study at KPJAYI, immersing herself in the Parampara tradition. Her teaching is precise and compassionate — she holds space for beginners and advanced practitioners alike, guiding the room through the full traditional count with steady rhythm and clear adjustments.
